Green University Islamic Community

The Green University Islamic Community (GUIC) is a non-profit, nonsectarian, and non-political organization dedicated to fostering the holistic development of students at Green University of Bangladesh (GUB).

Why GUIC

In today’s materialistic world, human beings often lose sight of their spiritual essence, focusing solely on worldly desires. This leads to moral decay, selfishness, and societal breakdown. GUIC strives to awaken the noble qualities inherent in humanity—qualities that make us Ashraful Makhluqat.

Vission
&Mission

Vission:

"Enlightenment with Education for Building a Healthy Society!"

GUIC envisions a world where university students become devout followers of the Qur’an and Sunnah while excelling in the modern world. Our goal is to build a healthy, harmonious society by producing leaders who balance faith (Deen ) and worldly responsibilities (Duniya ) effectively.

Mission:

"Nurturing the Mindset and Character of Young Individuals in a Fun and Relatable Manner to Become Productive Persons."

GUIC aims to guide, groom, and mentor young individuals in a friendly and approachable way. By empowering them with knowledge, skills, and values, we prepare them to lead purposeful lives and effect positive change in every aspect of society.

Founder's Vision

1. A spiritual haven where GUB students can understand Islam in its purest form and essence.

2. A dedicated forum created by young people to address psychological needs and help students define their purpose and identity.

3. A skill development platform focusing on Islamic values, leadership, and academic growth.

4. A source of inspiration for students to adopt the vision and way of life demonstrated by the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).
